Privacy Policy

Daily Drop: Sports Trivia · Effective 1 July 2026

The short version

Daily Drop does not collect, store, or share any personal information. There are no accounts, no sign-in, and no servers that receive your data. Everything the app remembers lives only on your device.

What the app stores on your device

To make the daily quiz work, the app saves a small amount of information locally on your phone only:

  • Your current and best streaks
  • Your quiz history and statistics (games played, accuracy, scores)
  • Which day’s quiz you have completed

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to us or anyone else. Deleting the app removes it.

Notifications

If you allow it, the app can schedule a daily local reminder to play. These reminders are generated and delivered entirely on your device. No push tokens or notification data are sent to any server. You can turn notifications off at any time in your device settings.

Analytics and tracking

The app contains no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking of any kind. We do not use cookies or device identifiers to track you.

Children’s privacy

Daily Drop does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. No information is gathered that could identify any user.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
